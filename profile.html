About

Rust & Chrome is a four-piece Southern rock outfit out of Memphis, TN. Drawing from the tradition of Lynyrd Skynyrd, Black Crowes, and The Allman Brothers, we bring high-energy, groove-driven sets to stages across the Southeast and beyond.

Currently on our Summer 2026 tour — 23 shows across 7 cities. We're active on BandAid and open to partnerships, gear loans, and fill-in opportunities in every city we play.
