How It Works

From crisis to curtain call in minutes.

Whether you need a drummer for one night or a last-minute guitar amp, BandAid has you covered.

01

Post Your Need

Tell us what you need — a bassist, a PA system, a drum kit, or a music store near your venue tonight.

02

Get Matched Locally

BandAid surfaces verified local bands and stores within your radius, ranked by availability and gear match.

03

Connect & Confirm

Chat directly, agree on details, and lock it in. BandAid handles the intro — you handle the music.

04

Play the Show

Hit the stage. Rate your partners after so the community stays tight and trustworthy.
